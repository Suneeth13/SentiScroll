Project Title: SentiScroll AI
Problem: Today's video apps (like YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, and TikTok) only care about how much time you spend watching. They don't know if you are actually enjoying the videos or if you are just tired and "doomscrolling." These apps are "emotionally blind"—they look at what you clicked in the past but ignore how you feel right now. For example, they might show stressful videos to someone who is already stressed. Also, current age filters are easy to trick and don't check who is actually sitting in front of the screen.

Solution: SentiScroll AI. Our system uses a camera and AI to understand the user's feelings and age in real-time.
- Face Reading: Uses real-time facial landmark detection (MediaPipe) to track Joy, Sadness, Stress, and Boredom.
- Age & Gender Detection: Automatically curates content based on the actual person in front of the camera (Kids, Teens, Adults).
- Eye Tracking: Monitors Blink Rate and Focus Score to detect digital eye strain and engagement.
- Agentic Reasoning: Integrates Google Gemini 1.5 Flash as an empathetic AI agent that generates context-aware support and manages the feed autonomously.
- Privacy: All biometric data is processed locally on-device via WebAssembly; no facial data is ever uploaded.

Technical Implementation:
- Core Tech: HTML5, CSS3 (Glassmorphism), Vanilla JavaScript, MediaPipe, face-api.js.
- AI Layer: Google Gemini 1.5 Flash API (Agentic Reasoning).
- Mobile Ready: Fully functional as a Progressive Web App (PWA) and includes a native Android wrapper (Capacitor).
- Security: Secure dynamic configuration for API keys via gitignored config layers.

Value: SentiScroll makes apps safer and healthier. Instead of just showing random videos, it "understands" how you feel. It helps prevent digital fatigue and ensures children only see safe content, significantly improving user mental health in the digital age.
