Problem: Today's video apps (like YouTube,Facebook,Instagram,snapchat and TikTok) only care about how much time you spend watching.
 They don't know if you are actually enjoying the videos or if you are just tired and "doomscrolling." 
 These apps are "emotionally blind"—they look at what you clicked in the past but ignore how you feel right now.
  For example, they might show stressful videos to someone who is already stressed. Also, current age filters are easy
   to trick and don't check who is actually sitting in front of the screen.

Solution: SentiScroll AI. Our system uses a camera and AI to understand the user's feelings and age in real-time.

Face Reading: It uses your camera to see if you are happy, sad, or bored.
Eye Tracking: It counts how often you blink and where you look to detect eye strain and tiredness.
Smart AI Agent: Using Gemini AI, a friendly digital assistant talks to you and changes the videos automatically
 if it sees you are unhappy or bored.
Privacy: All face data is processed on your device and is never uploaded to the internet.
Value: SentiScroll makes apps safer and healthier. Instead of just showing random videos, it "understands" 
how you feel. It helps prevent digital tiredness and ensures children only see safe content, making the internet better
 for your mental health.
